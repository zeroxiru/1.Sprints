Horse

There are an estimated 60 million horses in the world, combining wild and domesticated horses.

  1. Scientific Name : Equus caballus
  2. Average length : Light riding horses are typically 14–16 hands (1.42–1.63m)
  3. Average Lifespan : 25-30 years
  4. Habitat :Domesticated, or tamed, horses can live in almost any habitat, but wild horses prefer plains, prairies, and steppes for many reasons.

Horses have been domesticated for centuries and played crucial roles in transportation, agriculture, and warfare. With their incredible speed and endurance, horses are known for their athletic abilities. There are various horse breeds, each with unique characteristics and skills, catering to different purposes.
